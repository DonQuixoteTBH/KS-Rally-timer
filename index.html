Web Dashboard Guide

The Web Dashboard is your secondary sync terminal for desktop use.

  • Squad Unit: Link your device to the leader by entering the same Squad ID (e.g., T1).
  • Compass Selection: Automatically syncs your target direction and travel time from the leader's app.
  • Travel Time: Manual travel time inputs are saved locally in your browser for future sessions.
Pro-Tip: Enable Background Sync to keep the high-precision audio engine active while you are switched to your game window.

Android App Guide

The mobile app is the primary tactical interface for Squad Leaders.

  • Atomic NTP Sync: The app connects to pool.ntp.org to bypass local clock drift and provide sub-second accuracy.
  • Floating Overlay: Tap the "Grant Permission" button to enable the HUD that stays visible inside your game.
  • Leader Mode: When active, your Target UTC and Prep Minutes are broadcasted to all linked squad members.
Pro-Tip: Drag the RALLY button to any corner of your screen to prevent it from blocking your game's menu buttons.

APK Install Guide

Follow these steps to ensure the app runs with full permissions:

  1. Download the latest APK from the project source.
  2. Open the file and allow "Install from Unknown Sources" in your browser settings.
  3. If Play Protect shows a warning, select "Install Anyway" (the app uses sensitive overlay permissions).
  4. Battery Optimization: Search for "Battery Optimization" in your phone settings and set this app to "Don't Optimize".

Field Test Overview

Best practices for high-stakes coordination:

  • Discord Settings: Ensure your "Media Volume" is at 100%. The beep is optimized to cut through voice chat but requires media headroom.
  • Headset Latency: Bluetooth headsets have a "wake-up" delay. Background Sync keeps the connection "hot" to prevent missed beeps.
  • Atomic Verification: Squad members should call out the last 3 seconds (3.. 2.. 1..) to verify everyone is within the same 50ms window.
